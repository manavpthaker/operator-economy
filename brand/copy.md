### Section: How every episode works

**The thesis.** One kind of business you could build — stated plainly, no hype.
**The evidence.** Who's already making money at it, from solo operators to the venture-scale version. Every number carries its source, on screen.
**The stack.** The exact tools, what they cost, what they replace.
**The honest math.** Realistic ranges by effort level — and the failure modes. If we can't source it, we say it's an estimate. If it's a bad idea, we say that too.

### Section: The Blueprint library (email capture)

**H3:** Don't take notes. Take the document.
Every episode ships with an Operator Blueprint: the sourced working doc behind the video — evidence table, tool stack with costs, week-by-week playbook, every citation. Free, in exchange for your email. One email a week, no drip campaigns, unsubscribe anytime.

### Section: What this isn't

No income promises. No $997 course at the end of the funnel. No "secrets." This is a publication, not a pitch — the business model is you trusting the numbers enough to come back.

### About (footer / about page)

The Operator Economy is written and produced by an operator: product leader, founder (one exit), builder of the AI systems used to make this show. AI assists production; the analysis, judgment, and mistakes are human. Sources or it didn't happen.
