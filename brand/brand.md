Documentary rigor, operator credibility. Practical, specific, calm. Never: hype words ("insane", "secret", "crazy"), income promises, emojis in scripts, engagement bait. Always: sourced numbers, marked estimates, named failure modes.

Spoken intro: "Welcome to The Operator Economy." Sign-off pairs with tagline: "Build it. Own it. Operate it."
