01 · Brand foundation

One promise, documentary rigor

Positioning: The Operator Economy shows skeptical senior professionals (35–55) the businesses they could actually build now that AI collapsed the cost of building — real companies, sourced numbers, exact playbooks. Tagline: Build. Own. Operate.

Voice

  • Speak to the reader as "you"; the host is "I", used sparingly and accountably.
  • Sentence case everywhere. UPPERCASE is reserved for labels: SOURCE:, SHEET 02 OF 05.
  • Calm, specific, skeptical. Named failure modes. Honest difficulty.

Numbers

  • Every figure is sourced or explicitly marked as an estimate — never blended.
  • Every published number is set in Fragment Mono with a citation chip.
  • Units always attached: $5.9B, 31%, $500–5,000/mo.

Never say

  • Hype words: "insane", "secret", "crazy", "you won't believe".
  • Income promises: "$0 → $10K/mo", "quit your job".
  • "Hey guys, welcome back", fake scarcity, countdown urgency.
Sign-off: "Build it. Own it. Operate it."
02 · Color

Ink, paper, navy — and one accent

One palette. Any single composition contains Ink + Paper (or Navy) + at most one accent. Gold is the accent of the dark schematic world; Drafting Blue is the accent of the cream editorial world. Sage is not an accent — it exclusively means live / verified.

Gold mono passed on navy at chip size (6.4:1), so no lightening was needed there. The audit instead caught the paper-side deep gold at 4.1:1 — it was darkened to #7A5E24 (5.3:1). Chip labels never render below 10px.

03 · Typography

Two serifs, one job each

Boska is the display voice — drafting-blade contrast that only exists at scale. Zodiak 700 is the workhorse below it (the stress test showed Boska's hairlines sparkle under ~40px). Supreme carries body and UI. Fragment Mono carries every number and citation in its single 400 weight — scale, never synthetic bold, carries emphasis.

The 40px floor

Boska never renders below 40px. The moment a heading drops under the floor, it becomes Zodiak 700. No exceptions, including buttons and cards.

Italic ration

At most one italic phrase per composition — the emphasis beat ("actually", "Solo scale."). A second italic anywhere in frame is a violation.

Mono means published

If a number appears in Fragment Mono, it is sourced or marked as an estimate. Never set decorative or unverified figures in mono.

05 · Hard bans

Any one of these breaks the brand

  • Instrument Serif, Inter, or any default AI-startup look
  • Neon purples, pinks, electric blues; gradients of any kind
  • Glassmorphism, frosted cards, decorative blur
  • Shocked faces, red arrows, yellow circles
  • Income overlays ("$0 → $10K/mo") and money-stock imagery
  • Emoji on any brand surface
  • Countdown timers, fake scarcity, urgency patterns
  • Placeholder schematic nodes (no figure, "your tool here")
  • Numbers without a citation chip or estimate mark
  • Boska below 40px; synthetic-bold mono; more than one italic per frame
  • Radii above 3px; pills anywhere but status dots
  • More than one accent per composition
