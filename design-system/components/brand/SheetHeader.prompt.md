**SheetHeader** — a chapter/section divider styled as a drawing sheet. Used for video chapter cards, LinkedIn carousel sheets, and blueprint dividers.

```jsx
<SheetHeader sheet={2} total={5} title="Unit economics"
  subtitle="What one operator actually keeps after tooling." />
<SheetHeader sheet={1} total={5} title="The gap" onInk />
```

`onInk` for the dark video treatment. Numbers auto-pad to two digits.
