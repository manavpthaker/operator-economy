**CitationChip** — the channel's signature. Put one beside every published number: it's a compliance, trust, and brand asset the guru lane structurally can't fake.

```jsx
<CitationChip source="Sacra" date="Apr 2026" />
<CitationChip source="Creator reports" estimate />
<CitationChip source="Accenture FY2025" date="" onInk />   {/* video lower-third */}
```

`estimate` renders the gold "ESTIMATE" variant for marked estimates. `onInk` is the dark-surface lower-third used over video/hero.
