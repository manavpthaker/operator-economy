# Topic scoring rubric (100 pts)

| Factor | Pts | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Search demand | 25 | YT + Google volume for the idea and its query patterns ("how X makes money", "start X with AI") |
| Competition gap | 20 | Existing coverage depth — listicle-only coverage scores high; a dedicated quality breakdown scores low |
| Evidence availability | 20 | Can we name 2–3 companies with sourced revenue/economics? No evidence = no video |
| POV strength | 15 | Manav operator experience (hospitality, product, AI implementation). This is the moat — weight it |
| Affiliate/stack potential | 10 | Do the blueprint's tools pay recurring commissions? |
| Derivation richness | 10 | Does it yield strong LI posts/newsletter angles (career-transition resonance)? |

**Produce at ≥ 65. Archive below 50.** Re-score monthly against analytics (retention by section, capture rate by topic).
