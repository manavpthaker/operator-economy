# Kill criteria (set before launch, decided in advance)

**Commitment: 26 weeks of consistent uploads (~50 videos incl. shorts) before any kill/pivot decision.** Most failures quit months 4–6, right before compounding — so no vibes-based decisions inside the window.

## At week 26, KILL or PIVOT the format if ALL of:

- No video has broken 10k views
- Subscriber growth < 100/month
- Average view duration < 35%
- Email list < 300 (blueprint capture isn't working)

## DOUBLE DOWN if ANY of:

- 2–3 videos outperformed channel median by 10x → scale that sub-format
- Email capture rate > 1.5% of views → the blueprint asset works; consider gating more
- A Lane 2-style tool/workflow video outperforms consistently → spin-out decision (month 9–12)

## Budget guardrails

- Tooling ceiling: $300/mo. No freelancers before monetization.
- Domain/handle/brand spend: one-time, < $200.
- If total spend passes $2,500 with zero revenue signal → forced review regardless of week.

## Review cadence

Monthly analytics review (views, retention curves, capture rate) → adjust topic scoring weights in `topics/scoring.md`.
